Hi! I'm a 5th year Ph.D. student in School of Computer Science Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Motahhare Eslami and Haiyi Zhu.
I design and develop AI systems for high-stakes domains such as healthcare and the public sector, with a focus on integrating human values into data-driven technologies. My research explores how values are understood by diverse stakeholders and how they can be meaningfully embedded into the design and deployment of AI.
